Alumni




Our alumni are an integral part of Eta Chapter. Alumni involvement in the chapter is highly encouraged. Attending alumni and chapter events is a great to stay involved and make a difference in the chapter.

The Alumni Board of Eta Chapter of Beta Sigma Psi Serves in Three Major Functions:

  1. Ownership of the Chapter House
  2. Scholarships
  3. General Oversight of the Chapter



The Alumni Board relies on yearly dues and donations to provide yearly scholarships and to perform maintenance projects at the chapter house. Without alumni dues and donations, the chapter will struggle with any major house maintenance and recruitment. Alumni are always welcome at the chapter house. Stop by for a tour at any time and see how Beta Sigma Psi is improving its current members. To learn more about our Alumni Association, click here.

Giving and Dues




Thank you to all the alumni who have given dues and donations to the Alumni Chapter and to the Foundation. Your financial support is appreciated and very helpful for us to meet our goals and commitments.

1. General Chapter Donations




Although General Donations to our Rolla Alumni Chapter of Beta Sigma Psi are not tax deductible, they are needed. General donations are used for Chapter House upkeep and improvements that do not fall under IRS rules for educational area and functions of the house. These donations are essential to keeping the Eta Chapter House in good repair.
